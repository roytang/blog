We attended [PopQuiz 10th Anniversary: Origins Trivia Night](https://www.facebook.com/events/26867991436213417/).

In attendance: Me, Chry, Chery, Liz, Chi, Jet.

Only our second PQ in 2026, back at the familiar venue of Int/Ext

Rounds were based on the first six PopQuiz events held back in 2016! We attended 5 out of 6 of those events, and won four of them! I felt that gave us a good shot to win this one.

1. Horror - 4pts. Unsurprisingly our worst round, but we actually did better than I expected.
2. Magic and Mystics - 5pts. I missed an MCU question here about Agatha All Along!
3. Music (2016 hits backmasked) - 5pts x0 for failed gamble. I was useless here.
4. Superheroes (Casting couch) - 9pts x2 for power-up.
5. Disney movies (Gradual reveal - max 4pts per question) - 31pts!
6. 90s (audience votes on the topic for each question) - 9pts
7. Hail Mary: Emoji movie descriptions - 8pts

That failed gamble hit us hard, landing us tied for 5th place and a mere 8pts away from the winning team. If we had used the gamble in round 2 instead, we would have won the whole thing! Still, it was a fun trivia set and event (though there was a lot of shouting!)
