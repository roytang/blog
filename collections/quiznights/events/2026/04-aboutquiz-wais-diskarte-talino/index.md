We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night at an old venue: Saludos in Ortigas. On my way there, I passed by the construction site for the subway station and the guard jokingly told me it would be done soon, by 2032.

[Wais. Diskarte. Talino: A Street Smarts Quiz Night](https://www.facebook.com/events/2162604811162768/)

Round by round recap:

- R1: Commuting - 7pts
- R2: Street Food - 8pts
- R3: Palengke Smarts - 8pts (surprisingly higher than expected)
- R4: Basic Law - 5pts 
- R5: Practical Fashion - 8pts
- R6: Adulting - 4pts x2 for powerup (a terrible choice, apparently we were not as adult as we thought)
- Pabibo: valid IDs accepted when applying for PH passport renewal (successful wager of 7)

Our street smarts were only good enough for second place. We were 6 behind before the Pabibo round and we did hit our wager, but so did they!
