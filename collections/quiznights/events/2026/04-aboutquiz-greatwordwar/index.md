We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night at a new venue: 525th lounge in Estaban Abada near Katipunan! I only got lost a little bit while walking there!

[The Great Word War: A Word & Vocabulary Quiz Night](https://www.facebook.com/events/830294016109310)

Round by round recap:

- R1: Anagrams - 10pts x2 for power-up. Someone in our Discord said they expected us to perfect this round, and we did!
- R2: Definitions - 9pts
- R3: Word Origins - 9pts
- R4: Spelling - 3pts. So many obscure words!
- R5: Grammar - 5pts. Easily the hardest and most technical round of the night. 
- R6: Connections - 9pts
- Pabibo: Merriam-Webster words of the year - successful wager of 3.

I thought we would be losing because we were only ahead by 1 pt before the Pabibo round and we only wagered 3, but the other team wagered 8 and missed! So we ended up winning the event (just barely), earning us some extra fries!
