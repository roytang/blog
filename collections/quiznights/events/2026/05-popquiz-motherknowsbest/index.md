We attended [Mother Knows Best: a Mother's Day inspired Trivia Night](https://www.facebook.com/events/1390573132863253).

In attendance: Me, Chry, Chery, Liz, Chi.

Only our second PQ in 2026, back at the familiar venue of Int/Ext

Rounds were:

1. Mothers in TV - 4pts. The last 4 or 5 questions were all about local TV which we are very bad at!
2. RuPaul / Drag Race - 2pts. A terrible round for us, despite 2 members that were fans.
3. "Mother" in Music - 6pts. This was a quickfire round with 10 songs related to mothers and you had to identify what TV/movie/play it was from.
4. Mothers in Movies - 5ptx (2x for power-up) (not a great powerup)
5. Other Mothers - 6pts (+6pts for gamble). This was a hodgepodge of mother-related questions that didn't fit the other categories.
6. Mama Mo - 9pts. This was a quickfire round where we had to identify the mothers of local celebrity nepo babies.
7. (Hail Mary) Quickfire round of songs with "Mother" terms in the title, name the artist. - 6pts.

Given the terrible power-up and the poor first half, we got crushed, finishing a distant 5th out of 6 teams.
