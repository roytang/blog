We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night at an old venue: Saludos in Ortigas. This was [Only Konek: A Quiz Night inpired by the TV Show "Only Connect"](https://www.facebook.com/events/1514035503702299/)

I had never seen the show Only Connect before, so to prepare I watched exactly one episode. It's kind of like the NYT connections puzzle, which we do quite a bit of, so I hoped we could do well.

Attendance: Me, Chry, Jaime, Faith, Liz, Chi

Round by round recap:

- R1: What's next in the connection - 11pts
- R2: Missing vowels 1 - 8pts
- R3: What's next in the sequence - 6pts
- R4: Missing vowels 2 - 10pts
- R5: Word walls - 38pts
- R6: Missing vowels 3 - 10pts

The scoring here was wild and was tilted heavily towards the word walls. In this round, we got 3 different word walls of 16 words each (2 of them are shown in photos attached), and you had to sort them into 4 groups of 4 words each (like in NYT Connections). Each correct group of 4 gets you 4pts, and you can get a bonus 1pt if you correctly identify why those items are grouped together. That means each team could potentially score as much as 60pts here! 

Unfortunately it also means if you miss a group by even one word you lose 4pts! In our case for word wall #2 we put Perish and Scape Goat in the wrong groups (we had no idea what "Perish Song" is as apparently that is a pokemon thing). If we had swapped those 2 words in our groupings, we would have tied with the eventual first place team!

As it was, we instead finished a mere 3rd place out of 6 teams.
