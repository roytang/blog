We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night on labor day! It was at a new (for me) venue: 556 Lounge in Kapitolyo.

[General Trivial Pursuit 4.0: A General Trivia Quiz Night](https://www.facebook.com/events/1505781114543246/)

Round by round recap:

- R1: Geography - 3pts
- R2: Entertainment - 6pts
- R3: History - 5pts
- R4: Arts - 5pts 
- R5: Science - 3pts x2 for powerup (now we are on a streak of terrible powerup choices)
- R6: Sports and Leisure - 4pts
- Pabibo: Tony-award winning musicals (failed wager of 10, we only got 2)

We were behind by a whopping 10pts by the end of regulation, so we had no choice but to wager 10pts, but apparently none of the top 5 teams hit their wagers, so we still finished at 4th place.
