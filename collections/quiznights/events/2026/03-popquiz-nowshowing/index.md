Attendance: Me, Chry, Chery, Kim, Dan

We attended a Movies-themed PopQuiz at the Vault this Thursday. This is only our second quiz night for 2026! The venue was packed and the competition was fierce!

Rounds were:

1. Movie Plots - 6pts
2. Movie Awards - 3pts
3. Movie Quotes - 9pts (x2 for power-up)
4. Movie Characters - 10pts
5. Movie Soundtracks - 7pts (+7 for gamble)
6. Best Picture Winners (max 4 pts per question) - 30pts
7. (Hail Mary) Paul Thomas Anderson movies - 6pts

I don't think we did too poorly, but we finished a mere 7th out of 11 teams. The winning team got 110pts out of a possible 120 WTH!
