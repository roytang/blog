Our first quiz night for 2026! We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night at a new venue: HAKID drinks bar in Kapitolyo! The theme for this one was about fandoms.

[Event Page](https://www.facebook.com/events/1499200588394315)

Round by round recap:

- R1: Pokemon - 0pts. The questions were quite hard AND our team had no SME for this one
- R2: Lord of the Rings - 6pts 
- R3: DC Comics - 6pt. I have once again failed to properly spell Mxyzptlk and also failed to properly punctuate J'onn J'onzz
- R4: Marvel Comics - 8pts x2 for paimportante. I wanted to get a perfect score here, but they had a couple of deep cuts near the end. [Doctor Bong](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BhBy78aFZwE)?!?

We finished second place! Even if we had gotten the pabibo wager, the 1st place team was far enough ahead that it wouldn't have mattered. They were ahead of us by exactly one Pokemon round lol.
