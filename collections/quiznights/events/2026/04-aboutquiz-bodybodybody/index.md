We attended an AboutQuiz quiz night at a new venue: KozyKook Diner near Katipunan! I did not get lost this time.

[Body. Body. Body: A Trivia Night About the Human Body](https://www.facebook.com/events/1126173306357629)

Round by round recap:

- R1 Anatomy (powerup): 6pts (x2 for powerup)
- R2 Medical TV: 5pts. No Scrubs or House questions boo!
- R3 Iconic Body parts: 6pts
- R4 Diseases: 5pts
- R5 Human extremes: 6pts
- R6 Medical breakthroughs: 5pts
- R7 Pharmacy (2pts each): 12pts. This was a fun round where given 4 options, you had to identify which one was a generic name of a drug, and for an additional point, identify the commonality between the remaining 3 options.
- R8 Beauty Products: 6pts. We had to identify beauty products/establishments based on partially obscured logos/icons.
- Pabibo: 3-letter body parts - successful wager of 3 (got 9).

Before the Pabibo, we were ahead by 7, so getting 3 guarantees at worst a tie. For some reason second place only wagered a 6 though!

We won! We are on a two quiz night winning streak!
