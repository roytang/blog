Sa SM North may pila dapat ng "NIA/NPC" na jeep. Take that jeep and get off at Tandang Sora (ask the driver to drop you off). When you get there, take a jeep na "T.Sora Hiway", heading in the same direction as the NIA/NPC jeep. That jeep will turn right on Congressional and u-turn before turning right back to Tandang Sora. The Sea oil will be on the right side right after the bridge.

Pabalik, just take the T.Sora/Hiway jeep back the same route. You can get off at McDonald's (it will be on the left side). The NIA/NPC jeeps will be coming back from the corner near the 7-11 opposite the McDonald's. Wait for one that says "SM North/Trinoma" (not all of them go all the way back)
