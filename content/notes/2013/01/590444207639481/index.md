 <p>
  Grand Prix Singapore 2013 Super Sunday Series Changes
 </p>
 <p>
  The previously announced Sunday side events, Super Sunday Sealed and Standard, will be combined into a single series event: Super Sunday Series.
 </p>
 <p>
  The Super Sunday Series will feature two separate qualifying events, Super Sunday Sealed and Super Sunday Standard. The top  4 players of each event after the Swiss rounds will be entered into the Super Sunday Draft, which will determine the Super Sunday Series winner.
 </p>

  <br/>

  <br/>

  <br/>
  Super Sunday Standard - Standard, straight Swiss
  <br/>
  Super Sunday Draft - Return to Ravnica Booster Draft, Single Elimination
 </p>
 <p>
  Registration Fee:
  <br/>
  Super Sunday Sealed - SGD $35
  <br/>
  Super Sunday Standard - SGD $20
 </p>
 <p>

  Super Sunday Sealed - 9 am
  <br/>
