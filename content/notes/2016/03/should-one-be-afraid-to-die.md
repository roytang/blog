Someone on [quora](https://quora.com) asked:

> [Should one be afraid to die?](https://www.quora.com/Should-one-be-afraid-to-die/answer/Roy-Tang)


<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Fear can keep you alive, so yes. At the same time, you shouldn't let that fear hold you back from experiences that may be valuable or meaningful for you. Learn to understand and assess your fears so that you know when they are justified and when they are not</p></span>
