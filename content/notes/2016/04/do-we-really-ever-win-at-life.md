Someone on [quora]() asked:
> [Do we really ever win at life?](https://www.quora.com/Do-we-really-ever-win-at-life/answer/Roy-Tang)
<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Sure! The great thing about life is that you get to make your own goals. You get to define what winning means for you!</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Maybe winning for you means finding someone who loves you and you do it. You won!</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Maybe winning for you means helping out other people and you find a career that lets you do that. You won!</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Maybe winning for you means finishing college and working to see your younger siblings finish college too, and after years of hard work you do that. You won!</p><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Maybe winning for you means getting great answers on Quora then you find this answer and upvote it. You won!</p></span>
