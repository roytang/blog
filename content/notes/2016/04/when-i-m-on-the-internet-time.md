Someone on [quora](https://quora.com) asked:

> [When I'm on the Internet, time flies by. How can I stop that?](https://www.quora.com/When-Im-on-the-Internet-time-flies-by-How-can-I-stop-that/answer/Roy-Tang)


<span class="ui_qtext_rendered_qtext"><p class="ui_qtext_para u-ltr u-text-align--start">Be cognizant of your time. Choose how much time you want, set an alarm (your smartphone probably has one, you can't uninstall it), then (this is the most important thing): ACTUALLY STOP WHEN THE ALARM FIRES</p></span>
