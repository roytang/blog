I love January; January weather is the best in the Philippines. Mornings are comfortably nippy, enough to make you want to stay in bed, and it's rare to get afternoons with the typical punishing heat. The pleasant weather also makes this month the best for taking long walks outdoors on the usually hot city streets, though I haven't been able to do this recently (I tell myself it's due to the pandemic.) Unfortunately, January is halfway over, which means our pleasant weather days are nearing their end.

### The World

- Covid/Omicron continues to tear through the country. Cases are still rising in NCR, but rates are slowing down and apparently plateauing. This is mot likely due to hitting the limits of officially-reported testing more than anything else, since the positivity rates are still record highs.
- Tennis legend Novak "Novax" Djokovic is [to be deported from Australia](https://apnews.com/article/coronavirus-pandemic-novak-djokovic-sports-health-australia-2ca1c31f867c05a49e66a8b8f8849477) (which includes a three-year ban!) for not being vaccinated. He will be unable to play the Australian Open, where he won the mens' single event for the past 3 years. It was really stupid to not just get vaccinated in order to defend his streak. A fine example of just comeuppance for the rich and powerful.
