### My Week

After the positive antigen test two Fridays ago, I spent the entire week cooped up in my room. I was hoping my isolation period would end after the minimal 5 days, but my antigen test still came out positive last Wednesday. I was clear of fever by then but still had a bit of nasal congestion. Kept re-testing every two days and finally got a negative result today just after lunch today, so I am finally free!

One would think that being stuck in my room all day for a week would at least make me productive in terms of stuff I can do at my desk and/or computer, but honestly I just ended up spending most of my time reading comics and watching stuff and lying around.

I did manage to start doing [Inktober 2023](/tags/inktober2023/) though.
