#### Kaladesh Prerelease

[@ Neutral Grounds Eton Centris](/locations/4c529236384c76b0a0d0fc2a)

<time id="mtgkld-prerel">[2016-09-24 23:04] </time> Attended the Kaladesh prerelease at Neutral Grounds Eton Centris. It turns out I was bad at Kaladesh limited and finished 1-3. I found vehicles to be challenging to fight, especially if you are low on instant-speed removal. "They're like more annoying equipment," I said, because at least with equipment when a new creature you would need to pay the equip cost and wait a turn before they attack. With vehicles, every new creature comes with a new attack!

Added photos of the prerelease box and inserts for posterity.
