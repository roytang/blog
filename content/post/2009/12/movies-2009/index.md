#### Monsters vs Aliens

<time>[Mon, April 6, 2009, 7:19 p.m.]</time> Not spectacular, simple plot, a lot of predictable gags, but it's passable.

#### X-Men Origins: Wolverine

<time>[Thu, April 30, 2009, 11:06 p.m.]</time> X-Men Origins: Wolverine - mediocre. And Hugh Jackman is too tall to be Wolverine.

#### Star Trek

<time>[Sat, May 9, 2009, 8:52 p.m.]</time> heading out for star trek

<time>[12:27 a.m.]</time> I was at best a casual follower of the old series (and TNG), but I thought the Star Trek movie was quite good nonetheless

#### Terminator Salvation

<time>[Tue, June 2, 2009, 7:23 p.m.]</time> It's a Terminator movie, expect nothing more.

#### GI Joe: The Rise of Cobra

<time>[Sun, Aug. 9, 2009, 3:32 p.m.]</time> Quick GI Joe review: Very predictable/cliched plot, but action-packed and required far less suspension of disbelief than Transformers 2.

#### Echelon Conspiracy

<time>[Sat, Oct. 31, 2009, 8:46 p.m.]</time> Just finished watching Echelon Conspiracy. Action was ok, plot was a bit predictable, ending was lame.

#### The Taking of Pelham 123

<time>[Sat, Oct. 31, 2009, 11:31 p.m.]</time> Taking of Pelham 123: It has Denzel Washnigton and John Travolta in it, you'd expect a decent movie. You'd be right.

#### Taken

<time>[Sun, Nov. 15, 2009, 10:35 p.m.]</time> watching Taken
