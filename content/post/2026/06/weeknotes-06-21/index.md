### The World

- The Iran crisis has been resolved! Sort of. The US and Iran signed some kind of interim deal that kicks the question of nuclear disarmament down the road of further negotiations. It also gives Iran back control of the Strait of Hormuz AND promises them 300 billion USD of reconstruction money. This is largely seen as a capitulation by the Trump admin, though they are trying their best to spin it otherwise. Also, within the past 24h, Iran has said they are closing the Strait again after some ceasefire violations, just as the "further negotiations" are about to start. This all feels like we are not yet done.
- The PH Senate crisis has been resolved! For now. The president called a special session and a new proper Senate president and majority have been established, putting to bed APC's very short reign. Hopefully no more drama from them for a while, but I suspect things will heat up again once the impeachment trial kicks off.
