Had trouble getting through this one, similar to [Soul Music](/2026/01/soul-music/). It's not bad, but I find myself getting a bit tired of the whole "Death goes off on his extracurricular activities" schtick; it's entirely possibly the Death line of these books just doesn't agree with me. I also found Teatime a bit uninteresting as an antagonist. I do find Susan a much more interesting protagonist though. I think part of the reason I had so much trouble with this particular book is that I was so confused about what was going on until near the end; I kind of see it all come together around the same time Susan does.

I will probably try to do a Witches or Guards book after this one, to maybe get back to the kinds of plots that I liked more about the series.
