Most people need consistency more than they need intensity.



Intensity:

-run a marathon

-write a book in 30 days

-silent meditation retreat



Consistency:

-don't miss a workout for 2 years

-write every week

-daily silence



Intensity makes a good story. Consistency makes progress.
