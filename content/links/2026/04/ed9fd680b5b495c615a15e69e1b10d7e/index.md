Back in the late 90s I used to read a lot of anime/video game fanfiction. I remembered I used to read an insanely long/large multi-author collaboration crossover fanfiction where characters from different series and video games got together in some kind of giant fighting tournament, and that was MTCFF Ultra. The most astounding part about this is that they have a website that is still up and fairly functional considering the last entry was posted sometime in late 2001 or early 2002.
